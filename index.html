BACKGROUND & OVERVIEW

Human Activity Recognition and Prediction (HAR/HAP) have broad applications in healthcare, surveillance, and smart homes. However, capturing the complex patterns of human movement over time remains challenging.

Our project investigates Spatial-Temporal Graph Neural Networks (STGNNs), advanced AI models designed to better recognise and predict human actions from time-series data, potentially improving accuracy and real-world applicability.

The objectives of this work are to:

  1. Investigate the performance of state-of-the-art STGNN models against traditional deep learning models.
  2. Uncover what the STGNN models have learned about the dynamics of human activity.
  3. Develop a 3D environment for data augmentation.
